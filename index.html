About the Artist

Tool are a Los Angeles–formed progressive/alternative metal band known for complex rhythms, immersive visuals, and long-form songwriting. The lineup features Maynard James Keenan (vocals), Adam Jones (guitar), Justin Chancellor (bass), and Danny Carey (drums).

Breakthrough releases include Ænima (1996), Lateralus (2001), 10,000 Days (2006), and Fear Inoculum (2019). The band’s work frequently explores psychology, spirituality, and the tension between chaos and order.
